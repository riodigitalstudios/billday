What is BillDay?

BillDay is a privacy-first bill tracking and reminder app for Android. It helps households stay on top of recurring bills with timely reminders, spending insights, and country-specific comparison guides — all without requiring an account, cloud sync, or bank access. Everything stays on the device.

Key Features

What makes BillDay different

🔒

100% Local Storage

All data stored on-device via SQLite. No servers, no cloud, no data transmitted anywhere.

👤

No Account Required

Install and start tracking bills immediately. No sign-up, no email, no bank login.

🔔

Smart Reminders

Configurable notifications before bills are due — daily summary, individual alerts, or both.

📊

Spending Insights

Monthly breakdowns, category analysis, cost changes, and upcoming payment projections.

📋

Bill Guide

Country-specific comparison guides with real local providers (energy, broadband, insurance, mobile).

🌍

12 Languages, 10+ Markets

Localised for Ireland, UK, Austria, Germany, South Africa, Australia, US, Canada, and growing.

The Story

Why BillDay exists

I'm Edward, a solo Android developer from Ireland. I built BillDay because I kept getting caught out by bills — not because I couldn't afford them, but because they'd land on random dates across six different providers and I'd miss the odd one. Every bill tracker I tried wanted my bank login, a monthly subscription, or an account I'd never use again.

So I built what I actually wanted: a simple app that tracks when bills are due and reminds you before they hit. No account creation. No cloud sync. No data harvesting. Everything stays on your phone.

BillDay now supports 12 languages and has bill guides for Ireland, the UK, Austria, South Africa, and more — with real local providers, not generic templates. It's free, ad-supported, and I'm the only person who works on it.

Edward · Founder & Developer, Rio Digital Studios · Ireland
